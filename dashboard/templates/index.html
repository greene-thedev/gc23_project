About Our Fire Detection System

At Bunker Inc, we're dedicated to your safety. Our advanced fire detection system relies on state-of-the-art sensors to swiftly and accurately identify flames and smoke. Whether at home or work, our technology ensures rapid response and peace of mind, providing early warnings when you need them most.

  • Advanced Sensor Technology: Our system uses cutting-edge fire sensors for accurate and rapid fire detection.
  • Swift Response: We prioritize your safety with quick alerts when flames or smoke are detected.
  • Peace of Mind: Enjoy round-the-clock protection, whether you're at home or away.
  • Versatile Applications: Our system is adaptable to various settings, including homes, offices, and factories.
  • Reliability: Count on our dependable sensors to provide early warnings in critical situations
  • Open Source: Our commitment to open-source technology means you can trust our transparency and innovation.
  • User-Friendly: Experience an easy-to-use interface for effortless monitoring and control.
